Surrey Landscape Co

Website Design and Development

I worked with Surrey Landscape Co to deliver a simple responsive website containing the vital information about the company and its services.

A multi page webisite featuring a landing page, about page, services page and contact page. The client wanted a modern website that was easy to navigate and straight to the point.

One of the biggest challenges building this site was how to have the company logo appear large in the navigation bar without taking up too much space over the whole site. We came to the solution to have the large logo visible when the user is at the top of the page and to shrink it down when the page is scrolled.

The final product resulted in a simple but effective website that allows the user to view all sections of the site with minimal navigation.
