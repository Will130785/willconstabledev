Thai Orchid Takeaway

Website Design and Development

I worked with this local takeaway restaurant to build a modern responsive website that could be integrated with their EPOS system.

The site was built incorporating the style and colors or the companies brand. Featuring a carousel in the header section in order to display a range of photos to the user. The user can also drag or swipe each image in the carousel to bring up the next or previous photo.

The menu is linked to the restaurants EPOS system so customers can order directly through the website along with making payments.

The final product resulted in a website perfect for a modern day restaurant.
