Furnish

Website Design and Development

A modern and fully responsive website designed by myself for a furnishings company.

The site is simple but effective with some custom animations to add to the user experience. I wanted the site to give off a feeling of relaxation that would entice the customer to the store.

The color scheme was carefully selected to give the site the perfect ambience. The mild colors really help to give the user a calm, relaxed experience.

The final product resulted in a website perfect for a modern day furniture store.
